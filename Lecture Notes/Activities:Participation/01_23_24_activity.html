Five Nights At Freddy's
Five Nights at Freddy's is a video game series and media franchise created by Scott Cawthon. The first video game was released on August 8, 2014.
Minecraft™
Minecraft™ is a 2011 sandbox game developed by Mojang Studios and originally released in 2009.
Loftia
Loftia is a cozy, online multiplayer farming and life/social-sim game, inspired by games such as Stardew Valley and Animal Crossing, as well as ubiquitous MMOs such as World of Warcraft, Maplestory and more.
